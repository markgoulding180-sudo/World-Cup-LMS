Points Structure

The tournament now uses a points-based system. All players participate through the Final.

  • 🎯 Group Stage win = 2 points
  • 🎯 Round of 32 win = 4 points
  • 🎯 Round of 16 win = 6 points
  • 🎯 Quarter Final win = 8 points
  • 🎯 Semi Final win = 10 points
  • 🏆 Final win = 15 points

Winner = highest total points. Tiebreaker: most wins, then earliest entry.
