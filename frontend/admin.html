Points Structure

All players participate through the Final — no elimination. Highest points wins.

  • 🎯 Group Stage win = 2 points
  • 🎯 Round of 32 win = 4 points
  • 🎯 Round of 16 win = 6 points
  • 🎯 Quarter Final win = 8 points
  • 🎯 Semi Final win = 10 points
  • 🏆 Final win = 15 points

Tiebreaker: most wins, then earliest entry date.
