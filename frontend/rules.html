How to Play

Last Man Standing

World Cup Last Man Standing is a simple but exciting prediction game:

  1. Enter the tournament - Pay the entry fee to join
  2. Pick a team each round - Select one team you think will win
  3. Win = Advance - If your team wins, you go to the next round
  4. Lose = Eliminated - If your team loses or draws, you're out
  5. Last standing wins - The last player remaining wins the pot!

Important Rules

  • You can only pick each team once per tournament
  • Picks must be made before the round deadline
  • If all remaining players are eliminated in the same round, they share the pot
  • If multiple players reach the final, the pot is split among survivors
