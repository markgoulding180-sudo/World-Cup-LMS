How to Play

World Cup 2026 Points Competition

Predict winners and earn points! The player with the most points at the end wins the pot!

Entry

  • Entry Fee: £30 per player
  • Max Players: 100
  • Winner Takes All: Full pot to the player with the most points

Points System

Earn points every time your pick wins! More points are awarded in later rounds:

Group Stage 2 points
Round of 32 4 points
Round of 16 6 points
Quarter Finals 8 points
Semi Finals 10 points
Final 15 points

Group Stage (9 Picks)

  • 3 Picks per Matchday: Pick 3 teams to win on each matchday (1, 2, 3)
  • 27 Matches Total: 9 picks across 3 matchdays covering all 72 group games
  • Each team once: You can only use each team once across all 9 picks
  • Earn Points: Get 2 points every time your pick wins
  • No points for draws/losses: Only winning picks earn points

Knockout Stage

  • 1 Pick per Round: Round of 32, Round of 16, Quarter Finals, Semi Finals, Final
  • Points increase each round: 4pts → 6pts → 8pts → 10pts → 15pts
  • Same rules: Earn points when your pick wins, no points for losses

How to Win

  • The player with the most total points at the end wins
  • If multiple players tie for first, the pot is split equally
  • Track your progress on the leaderboard - rank is based on total points

Important Notes

  • Picks must be submitted before the match kicks off
  • Extra time and penalties count for knockout matches
  • Results updated automatically from official sources
  • You can see your total points, wins, and rank on your dashboard
