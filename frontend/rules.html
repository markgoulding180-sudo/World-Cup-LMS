How to Play

World Cup 2026 Last Man Standing

Predict winners and be the last player standing to win the pot!

Entry

  • Entry Fee: £30 per player
  • Max Players: 100
  • Winner Takes All: Full pot to the last standing player

Group Stage (9 Picks)

  • 5 Lives: Each player starts with 5 lives
  • 3 Picks per Matchday: Pick 3 teams to win on each matchday (1, 2, 3)
  • 27 Matches Total: 9 picks across 3 matchdays covering all 72 group games
  • Each team once: You can only use each team once across all 9 picks
  • Lose a Life: If your team loses or draws, you lose 1 life
  • All Lives Lost = Eliminated: When you hit 0 lives, you're out

Knockout Stage

  • 1 Pick per Round: Round of 32, Round of 16, Quarter Finals, Semi Finals, Final
  • Lives Carry Over: Lives from group stage continue into knockouts
  • Same Rules: Lose a life if your pick doesn't win

How to Win

  • Be the last player with lives remaining
  • If multiple players survive to the end, the pot is split
  • If all remaining players are eliminated in the same round, they share the pot

Important Notes

  • Picks must be submitted before the match kicks off
  • Extra time and penalties count for knockout matches
  • Results updated automatically from official sources
